CNIB




Opportunity


The Canadian National Institute for the Blind (CNIB) has the opportunity to partner
with UV-Cense Technologies for a mutually beneficial collaboration to address the
concerns aound touch stigma within the COVID environment and beyond. UV-Cense developed
three products exclusively to resolve the concerns provided by the CNIB. Initial
discussions between Dr. Mahadeo Sukhai, the Director of Research and Chief Inclusion
and Accessibility Officer for the CNIB, and UV-Cense on have yielded positive results
and set the foundation for collaborating to reduce touch stigma and promote societal
well-being (personal communication, Friday, February 5, 2021).



Benefit To CNIB


Partnering with UV-Cense will provide the CNIB exclusive branding by featuring the CNIB
logo on all UV-Cense products. Moreover, UV- Cense will display the CNIB on its website
which will encourage societal awareness of the cause and promote shared missions and values.
This partnership will provide supplemental revenue streams for the CNIB through royalties
which can be used to further support CNIB initiatives.



Benefit to UV-Cense


UV-Cense is a for-profit company that values equity, inclusion, and the betterment of society.
A partnership would allow both parties to work together to raise awareness for the impact
of touch stigma. With this strong collaboration, the CNIB’s support would provide further
credibility to the products. Lastly, the partnership will allow UV-Cense to meet the three
pillars of the triple bottom line, people, planet, and profit.



Why It Will Benefit Society


This collaboration will not only benefit both UV-Cense and the CNIB, but will play an integral
role in improving the welfare of society as a whole by helping to reduce the transmission of
COVID-19. UV-Cense products will reduce illnesses and transmission of other viruses, therefore,
producing a healthier society and reducing the cost of sick days for employers. Furthermore,
it will facilitate deeper cleaning protocols that will help keep all Canadians safer and
protected from avoidable risks.
