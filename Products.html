Products


Enclosed


The primary system will contain a UV-C light that is enclosed by a plastic shell
which will be utilized to sanitize small objects that can be covered entirely, such
as accessibility buttons.






Wand


The second system will be composed of a UV-C light, enclosed by a bar, that is object
facing and runs on tracks to sanitize large/flat surfaces, for example, elevator button
panels.







On Tracks


The third system, addressing considerations outlined by Dr. Sukhai, will be a UV-C wand
that is used by trained sighted employees and sight loss individuals to sanitize walls
and table corners.
